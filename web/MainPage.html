In this emerging world of computers almost all manual system are automated and computerized but maximum of them are so complex that a common user is unable to operate it. We are developing ‘Online Hardware Shops Management System’ to model an easy-to-use interface that will let hardware shopkeepers to maintain their shops.



Shopkeepers these days keep record of their stock availability by manually checking and noting down everything. The automation and computerization of these actions can significantly reduce the time consumed in performing them. This also reduces the burden on the shopkeepers as they will have a user-friendly GUI with which they can check on particular things and take action on them accordingly.



The Online Hardware Shops Management System will simplify the ordering, stocking and record maintaining of the shopping process and the shops themselves. Shopkeepers can keep customers better informed about the products which will increase overall profitability. Customers will have the option of door-step delivery which will ease the process of shopping for large hardware.
